Revolve Waterbottle

The goal of this project was to design a water bottle that fits into the daily routine like a jigsaw piece into a puzzle. Keeping the design accessible by staying true to a functionalist vision, avoiding any unnecessary gimmicks.

The design is focused around one simple innovation: being able to open the bottle with one hand, thus allowing one hand to be free while opening, drinking and closing. And because the cap can't be easily separated from the bottle, you never have to be afraid of losing it.

The design features a large opening allowing for easy refilling and adding ice, fruit and herbs to your water. A filter keeps unwanted stuff like seeds out of your mouth.

Process

Multiple prototyping iterations for optimizing ergonomics. The wooden model simulates the weight of a filled bottle.

Testing different filter shapes for best water flow.

Testing prototype in real life contexts.

Revolve Waterbottle is designed and developed in collaboration with Maarten Roos
