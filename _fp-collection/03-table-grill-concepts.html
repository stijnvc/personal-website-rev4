Table Grill Concepts

Internal electrical and thermal components for consumer table grills are standardized and cheap. Value can almost exclusively be created by designing a unique form factor. In these concepts I focussed on designing three distinct form factors by playing with the overal shape of the housing and by thinking of different layouts for the cast aluminium grill surface.

Designed at Studio Mango in Breda
